Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Health Tracker ("the App"), developed by Thilak Pollampally, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your name and email address (from your Google account)
  • Your profile photo (from your Google account)

When you use the App, you voluntarily provide:

  • Health data: height, weight, date of birth, gender, target weight
  • Daily food logs, calorie entries, water intake, step counts
  • Weight history entries

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Create and manage your personal health tracking account
  • Calculate your personalised daily calorie targets
  • Display your progress, trends, and weekly summaries
  • Sync your data across your devices

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal data to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your health data is stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database provider. Each user's data is protected by Row Level Security — meaning only you can access your own data. No other user or administrator can view your personal health logs.

4. Google Sign-In

We use Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication. We only request basic profile information (name, email, photo). We do not access your Gmail, Google Drive, contacts, or any other Google services.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you have an active account. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
